About Team Shop Sum
Welcome to Shop Sum, your go-to solution for hassle-free payment calculations and transactions.
~ Our Mission ~
At Shop Sum, our vision is to provide small vendors and roadside stalls a simple, efficient, and secure way to handle payments, hence the name "Shop Sum".
Everyone should have access to modern payment solutions without the need for complex procedures or paperwork.
~ What We Offer ~
  • Calculate Totals Easily:
    Quickly sum up the cost of all items in an order with our intuitive interface.
  • Generate QR Codes for Payments:
    Create a custom QR code linked to your UPI ID for the total amount, which can be paid using any UPI app.
  • Secure and Private:
    We value your privacy. No bank details or KYC information are required.
  • Fast and Convenient:
    Save time and reduce errors with our seamless payment process, tailored specifically for small businesses.
~ Why Choose Shop Sum? ~
  • User-Friendly:
    Our app is designed with simplicity in mind, ensuring that even those with minimal technical skills can use it effortlessly.
  • No Hidden Fees:
    We believe in transparency, which is why our services come with no hidden charges.
  • Reliable Support:
    Our dedicated support team is always here to help you with any questions or issues you might have.
