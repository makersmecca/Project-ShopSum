Privacy Policy
We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our services.
1. Information We Collect
Google Sign-In
  • Gmail ID: If you Sign in using Google, we store your Gmail ID.
  • Username and Display Name: We read and store your username and display name linked to your Google account.
  • Profile Picture: We read and store your profile picture linked to your Google account.
Email Sign-Up
  • Email ID: If you sign up using your email ID, we store the email ID you provide.
  • Username: We store the username you provide during sign-up.
Phone Number Sign-Up
  • Phone Number: If you sign in using your phone number, we store the phone number you provide.
  • Username: We store the username you provide during sign-up.
UPI ID
  • UPI ID: After successful sign-up, we link and store the UPI ID you provide inside the app.
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
  • Provide and improve our services.
  • Authenticate your account.
  • Facilitate transactions through the UPI ID you provide.
  • Personalize your experience based on your profile information.
3. Data Storage
All the data we collect is stored in Google's Firebase database, ensuring robust security measures to protect your information.
4. Sharing Your Information
We do not share your personal information with any third parties, unless we are legally obligated.
5. Your Rights
You have the right to:
  • Access and update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out of certain data collection practices.
6. Security
We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the date at the top will reflect the last update.
8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@shopsum.com.
9. Accessibility
  • This privacy policy is publicly accessible at ShopSum Home.
  • The privacy policy is hosted and accessible on the domain of our website.
  • The privacy policy is accessible from the app’s home page.
10. Google User Data
We only use Google user data in the ways described in this Privacy Policy:
  • We access and use your Gmail ID, username, display name, and profile picture solely for authentication and personalization purposes within the app.
  • We do not share Google user data with any third parties.
  • We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Google APIs Terms of Service.
